About Me

Chloe Blumer

Hello! My name is Chloe, I am a student interested in how emerging technologies shape everyday life, particularly in the transportation industry. This newsletter was created as part of my coursework to explore how artificial intelligence is being applied in modern vehicles.

My focus is on real-world AI systems that are shaping the automotive industry with attention to safety, regulation, and how different companies approach automation. By comparing industry strategies, I aim to better understand how applied AI impacts both innovation and public trust.

This site serves as a collection of analytical articles examining current developments in AI-powered driver assistance systems.
